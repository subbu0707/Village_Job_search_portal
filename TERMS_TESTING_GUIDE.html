✨ What's New

1. Mandatory Terms Acceptance

Users MUST check the "I accept the Terms and Conditions" checkbox before logging in. Without checking it, the login button won't work.

2. Beautiful Terms Modal

Clicking on "Terms and Conditions" link opens a professional popup showing all 12 sections of legal terms in the user's selected language.

3. Multi-Language Support

All terms are fully translated into 4 languages: English, Hindi (हिंदी), Telugu (తెలుగు), and Tamil (தமிழ்).

4. Database Tracking

When users accept terms, the system records the acceptance with a timestamp in the database for legal compliance.

🧪 How to Test

Step 1: Open the Login Page
  • Navigate to http://localhost:5173/login
  • You should see the login form
Step 2: Check the New Checkbox
  • Look for the gray box with checkbox near the bottom of the form
  • It says: "☐ I accept the Terms and Conditions *"
  • The "Terms and Conditions" text is a clickable link
Step 3: Try Without Checking
  • Enter email: employer@example.com
  • Enter password: password123
  • Don't check the checkbox
  • Click "Login to Account"
  • ❌ You should see error: "You must accept the Terms and Conditions"
Step 4: Open the Terms Modal
  • Click on the "Terms and Conditions" link
  • A beautiful modal should popup
  • Scroll through all 12 sections
  • See the yellow "Important Notice" box at bottom
Step 5: Accept Terms
  • In the modal, click "✓ I Accept Terms & Conditions" (green button)
  • Modal closes
  • Checkbox is now automatically checked ✓
Step 6: Login Successfully
  • Checkbox is now checked ✓
  • Click "Login to Account"
  • ✅ Login should succeed
  • You'll be redirected to the dashboard
